Everything you need to know about working with Response Reconstruction Services.

Yes. We are fully licensed, insured, and bonded. We carry general liability insurance, workers' compensation coverage, and builder's risk insurance. We provide proof of insurance before any work begins and maintain comprehensive coverage throughout every project for your peace of mind.
We work on a milestone-based payment structure. An initial deposit is due at contract signing, with subsequent payments tied to completed phases of work (e.g., demolition complete, rough-in complete, finish work complete). Final payment is due upon your satisfactory completion walk-through. This ensures you only pay for work that's been completed to your satisfaction.
